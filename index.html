About Me

I'm Varand Vartanian (AKA TheBitBrine) an 18 years old armenian boy who lived in Iran for most of his life. Currently i am living in Yerevan, Armenia. I've moved to here alone to develope myself in a better place, and i've been here for the past 2 years and i am almost on schedule with my plans :D

I almost always try to escape the typicality, the social norm, and traditions. I'm trying to not follow the way that my partents and relatives went and i'm trying to discover my own way, gain my own experiences, and even shine light to other people's path of life. I tied and i will keep trying to make people escape the typicality and make their own, who have the potential of applying it and people who haven't based their lives on bullshit yet.

I've always wanted to learn whatever i want, whenever i want, and however i want. So i've skipped the school a lot and stayed home and learned whatever i like, from programming to audio editing, from electronics to hijacking internet from neighbors, from torrenting movies to... you get the picture :D

Radio IdleYerevan

The Radio IdleYerevan is a collaborative project with some of my friends, who really hate radio stations playing pop music 24/7. So we started a small online radio which plays only good music, it's not quite there yet but we're still working on it. If you want us to have your music on the radio please head to contact page and message us on your desired platfrom.

TV Shows Directory

The TV Shows Directory is a file server running on a separate server which is all automated, the only thing that i have to do when i want a whole series is to type in the name. It automatically grabs the latest torrents of the episodes within the given file size and quality and renames them and put them into their folders all together. But unfortunately i can't add everyone's series so there is only ones that i watch. Like Rick And Morty ;)

Public File Directory

It's just a download center for rare random files. Some of these are way so rare that i had to ask people on reddit if anyone of them had it lol. If you're looking for something and can't find it feel free to email me and ask if i could find that file for you :D

Autonauts File Mirror

This is just a file mirror for legacy releases for one of my beloved indie games, Autonauts. I made this because their hosting provider didn't allow them to keep more than a few releases, and as every version is kinda different and special i decided to keep them all in my warm and cozy server forever :D
